Privacy Policy

Aqua - Explore the Ocean Depths
Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Welcome to Aqua ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our ocean exploration and AI image generation application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Apple ID Credentials: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. We use this solely for authentication purposes.
  • Profile Information: Your display nickname and profile avatar photo, which you may optionally set within the App.
  • User-Generated Content: Text prompts you enter to generate AI ocean images and any bookmarks or favorites you create.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Token balance, number of images generated, content reading progress, and category preferences — all stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version for compatibility and performance optimization.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or health data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity via Sign in with Apple and maintain your login session.
  • AI Image Generation: Your text prompts are sent to our AI service provider (Together AI) to generate ocean-themed images. Prompts are processed in real time and are not stored on external servers after generation is complete.
  • Personalization: To display your profile information, track your reading progress, and manage your bookmarked content.
  • In-App Purchases: To process MarineCoin token purchases through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple; we do not collect or store your payment information.
  • App Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the overall experience.

3. Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage:

  • User Preferences: Stored in local device storage (UserDefaults), including your token balance, reading progress, bookmarks, dark mode preference, and profile settings.
  • Generated Images: Saved locally in the App's documents directory on your device. A maximum of 100 images are retained, with older images automatically removed.
  • Avatar Photos: Stored locally in the App's documents directory.

We do not currently offer cloud backup or sync services. Your data resides solely on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Together AI

We use Together AI's FLUX.1-schnell model to generate ocean-themed images from your text prompts. Your prompts are transmitted securely to Together AI's servers for processing. Please refer to Together AI's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

4.2 Apple Services

We use Sign in with Apple for authentication and Apple's In-App Purchase system for MarineCoin transactions. These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App may request access to:

  • Camera: To take a profile photo for your avatar.
  • Photo Library (Read): To select an existing photo as your profile avatar.
  • Photo Library (Write): To save AI-generated ocean images to your device's photo library.

These permissions are optional and requested only when you initiate the relevant action. Photos are processed entirely on your device.

6. Microphone and Speech Recognition

The App may request access to your microphone and speech recognition services to allow voice input for describing ocean scenes. Audio data is processed by Apple's on-device speech recognition framework and is not transmitted to our servers.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information:

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption.
  • Authentication is handled through Apple's secure Sign in with Apple framework.
  • Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features.

8. Children's Privacy

Aqua is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Account: Use the "Delete Account" option in your Profile to permanently remove all your data from the device, including your profile, generated images, bookmarks, reading progress, and token balance.
  • Log Out: You can log out at any time from the Profile screen.
  • Individual Content: You can delete individual generated images or remove bookmarks at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App. Your continued use of Aqua after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
